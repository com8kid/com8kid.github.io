About Me

I'm a San Francisco based producer. My love for producing started when I was 14, I didn't know any software, just a guitar and a sheet of paper with tabs on it. Fast forward 2 years, a friend of mine tells me about Ableton Live, seeing the complex interface I immediately backed out, because softwares like that would take me years to learn, but when I saw him making really good music, I thought I should give it a go. I started learning the software, from Internet,friend etc. And now, after 5 years of learning (and I'm still learning), I have started making music professionally and releasing them.

Although I don't have access to proper hardware (just my Monitor Headphones) and also I have limited Music Theory knowledge,

My love for producing has never faded, and will never fade
